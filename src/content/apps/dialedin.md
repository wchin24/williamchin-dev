## About DialedIn

DialedIn is for coffee people who want to stop guessing and start dialing in. Every bag of beans is different — DialedIn helps you track what works so you can repeat it.

Log grind size, water temp, dose, brew time, and tasting notes for every session. Build a history. Spot patterns. Nail the cup.

Your brew data stays on your device. Always.

### Key Features

- Log every brew: grind size, water temp, dose, brew time, and more
- Rate sessions and track tasting notes per bean
- Equipment tracking across brewing methods
- Full brew history with filtering and comparison
- Optional account for backing up profile preferences across devices

### Why I Built It

I kept nailing a great cup one morning and losing it the next because I forgot what I changed. I tried spreadsheets. Too much friction. I wanted something fast, simple, and built for how I actually brew — so I built it myself.

### Technical Details

- Built with React Native and Expo
- Available on iOS and Android
- Local-first: brew data never leaves your device
- Optional Supabase-backed account for preference sync
- Donation-supported via Buy Me a Coffee — free forever, no paywalls
